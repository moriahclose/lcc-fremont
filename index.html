Who we are

Lighthouse Covenant Church is a CREC endeavor to bring faithul worship of God in His prescribed manner to the San Fancisco Bay Area.

We are currently in a time of transition folowing our pastor's departure and under the authority of Trinity Presbyterian Church in Aptos in order submit ourselves to rightful church authority.

We will also be expanding this site to include more detailed doctrinal statements which are specifically relavent to our region. Available IMMEDIATELY is our doctrinal confession, The Three Forms of Unity, starting on page 171.
