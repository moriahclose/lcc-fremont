Who We Are

Lighthouse is a group of families worshipping together in the San Francisco Bay Area. We are working towards becoming a church in the Community of Reformed Evangelical Churches (CREC).

Learn About the CREC

We meet every Sunday for fellowship and liturgical worship. Our doctrinal confession comes from The Three Forms of Unity: The Belgic Confession, The Heidelberg Catechism, and The Canons of the Synod at Dordt.

Read The Three Forms of Unity

We are currently in a time of transition following our pastor's departure. In order submit ourselves to rightful church authority, we are under the authority of Trinity Covenant Church in Aptos, California.
